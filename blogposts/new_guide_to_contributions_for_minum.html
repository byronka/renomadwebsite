New Guide to Contributions for Minum

September 8, 2024

I keep reading essays (see notes below) how simplicity should be favored over complexity. That microservices and Kubernetes are overkill for most projects. Why choose undue complexity needlessly, why not keep things simple?

For that reason, Minum, began development in December of 2021, embracing simplicity as its guiding star. Version one was released almost a year ago, and the project has entered a state of stability. The focus is providing a zero-dependency web framework for developers of monolithic, server-side-rendered web applications.

I have recently added a guide on making contributions to the project, if any of this piqued your interest. I would certainly value the insight of other practitioners.
